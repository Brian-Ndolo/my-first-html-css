Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  2. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form,then flip until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup or your favorite toppings
